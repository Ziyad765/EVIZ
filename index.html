About Us

EVIZ Industrial Zone is a private industrial estate located in Palakkad, Kerala, offering world-class infrastructure for modern industries. Designed to support manufacturing, logistics, and innovation, the estate emphasizes sustainability, operational efficiency, and investor-friendly policies.

Our Vision

To build a smart, sustainable, and globally connected industrial destination that drives innovation and economic growth.

Our Mission

  • • To provide ready-to-build industrial plots with robust infrastructure
  • • To promote clean and green industrial practices
  • • To support Make in India and global manufacturing initiatives
